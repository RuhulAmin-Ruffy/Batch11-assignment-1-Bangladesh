Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving growth through creativity, advanced technologies,and entrepreneurship to build a sustainable and prosperous future.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly practices and renewable resources to ensure long-term prosperity and environmental protection.

Infrastructure

Building robust and modern facilities to support economic growth, connectivity, and improved quality of life for all.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals through education, skills, and opportunities to drive progress and create an inclusive society.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Mohammad Yunus envisions a New Bangladesh as a beacon of hope and progress, founded on the principles of zero poverty, zero unemployment, and zero net carbon emissions. Through the power of social business, he aims to transform economic systems into engines of inclusivity, sustainability, and shared prosperity. His vision emphasizes empowering individuals through education, skills, and opportunities, fostering innovation to address global challenges, and building a society rooted in equality and dignity. By addressing systemic issues and promoting environmental harmony, Dr. Yunus dreams of creating a model nation that inspires the world to embrace peace, sustainability, and collective well-being.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
